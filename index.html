About L-P Venture Group

We are a global consulting firm with specialized divisions serving international clients across multiple industries.

Trusted International Advisors

Founded in 2010, L-P Venture Group has grown into a respected international consulting firm with offices in Paris, Lima, and Miami. Our team combines local expertise with global perspective to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

We specialize in bridging business operations between Europe and Latin America, with particular focus on France and Peru. Our three specialized divisions work synergistically to provide comprehensive solutions for international businesses.

Our approach is tailored, data-driven, and results-oriented. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions, but rather in developing strategies that align with your specific business objectives and market conditions.
